Course Overview

The core competencies of communication, programming, graphic design, as well as digital media and the internet, are all covered under the term "digital arts." Additionally, it gives students the opportunity to develop their intellectual, technical, aesthetic, and professional storytelling abilities across various mediums. New Media Art has evolved into a synthesis of artistic abilities and technological know-how. Its function is to aid you in securing the technology side necessary to strengthen your artistic abilities. You'll be involved in publishing computer software, making movies, television, radio, newspapers, magazines, and video games. Applying critical thinking and creative strategies to contemporary theory and art-making techniques are key components of new media art. Designers introduce everyone to a variety of technologically based techniques, such as the creation of interactive installations, bio-art, fast prototyping, and Internet art.

New Media Course Offerings:

Animation

As an animator, you have to merge the latest digital technology with your limitless and compelling storytelling. WIth your skillset in both commercial and fine-art, you are responsible for creating a series of images known as frames, to simulate movement. Your duties include working with creative professionals including storytellers, designers and voice actors to produce films, video games or graphics for commercials and other forms of TV entertainment.

Film

With a keen interest in the world of film, you combine motion picture techniques with digital technology to create effective cinema. In the process, you are set to produce versatile and important elements in making a film: directing, producing, screenwriting, editing, sound mixing, cinematography, and production design.

Multimedia Arts

As a multimedia artist, your skills in graphics technology programs will come in handy as you create design and effects for digital media. This field combines your core skills in communication, interface design, and programming for various user-driven applications such as digital and print media, web content and design, mobile content, interactive games and content, and exhibit installations.

Photography

As a Photographer, you have a knack for seeing things in a unique or artistic way. It is about your creative vision, how you see the world through the lens of your eye. Your goals are to have an intuitive mastery of light, a deep grounding in technique, and a genuine connection with the subject.
